Who are you?

Gordon McLachlan, Managing Director and co-founder of Primate.

Why did you want to have a baby?

To be honest, it was really the Wife who wanted one.

How do you feel about becoming a father?

Terrified. Unprepared. Too young. Like my life is about to end. Honestly, I don’t know anyone who likes have children so I can’t say the notion appeals (but then neither does dying alone, so there’s that). Hopefully, I’ll prove that these folk are just a bunch of whiny martyrs.

A deepeer understanding of myself, my child, and what it means to be a father. Also lots of new business for Primate.
