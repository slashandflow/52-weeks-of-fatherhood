52weeks of fatherhood

Hellobaby

In the summer of 2015 two men who work for the same company became fathers for the first time. Petrified, unprepared, and barely able to look after themselves, they decided to document and contrast their individual experiences of fatherhood every week for a year.

These are their stories.

Steve

Who are you?

Steve Brown. Husband. Daddy. Creative.

Why did you want to have a baby?

Nurturing a little life while watching them grow will be really fun and rewarding experience.

How do you feel about becoming a father?

Both excited and apprehensive. Will I be a good dad? Am I ready for this? How do you know what the heck you’re doing? All that aside, I’m really looking forward to it.

What are you hoping to get out of this blog?

A wonderful memory box of my daughter's first year, something for her to look back on and enjoy. Plus a movie deal. I’ve always wanted Bradley Cooper to play me in a major motion picture.

Gordon

Who are you?

Gordon McLachlan, Managing Director and co-founder of Primate.

Why did you want to have a baby?

To be honest, it was really the Wife who wanted one.

How do you feel about becoming a father?

Terrified. Unprepared. Too young. Like my life is about to end. Honestly, I don’t know anyone who likes having children so I can’t say the notion appeals (but then neither does dying alone, so there’s that). Hopefully, I’ll prove that these folk are just a bunch of whiny martyrs.

What are you hoping to get out of this blog?

A deepeer understanding of myself, my child, and what it means to be a father. Also lots of new business for Primate.
