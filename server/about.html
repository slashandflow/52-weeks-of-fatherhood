Hellobaby

Who are you?

Steve Brown. Husband. Daddy. Creative.

Why did you want to have a baby?

Nurturing a little life while watching them grow will be really fun and rewarding experience.

How do you feel about becoming a father?

Both excited and apprehensive. Will I be a good dad? Am I ready for this? How do you know what the heck you’re doing? All that aside, I’m really looking forward to it.

What are you hoping to get out of this blog?

A wonderful memory box of my daughter's first year, something for her to look back on and enjoy. Plus a movie deal. I’ve always wanted Bradley Cooper to play me in a major motion picture.
